Energizing the Future with our Community-based Design (CBD) approach

EnergizeX is built on the principles of community-based design, which emphasizes stakeholder participation and co-creation. This approach is reflected in the platform's support for community-driven projects and the development of co-ownership and community-governed models for project development.

Community engagement

Involving conducting focus groups, workshops, and other forms of engagement to involve community members in the design process and to gather input on their needs and preferences.

Co-design

Working with community members to co-create solutions that meet the needs and preferences of the community. This could include techniques such as participatory mapping, visioning exercises, and collaborative problem-solving.

Systems thinking

Using a systems-based approach to understand the interconnections between different components of the energy service system, and how they impact the community.

Participatory learning

Involving community members in the monitoring and evaluation of the energy service system and using their feedback to inform ongoing design and implementation.

Policy co-creation

Working with community members and other stakeholders to co-create policies and regulations that support the implementation and operation of the energy service system.
